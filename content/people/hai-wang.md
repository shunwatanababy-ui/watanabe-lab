### Hai Wang

**Postdoctoral Researcher**,

Emergent Molecular Electronics Research Team (Watanabe Lab)

RIKEN Center for Emergent Matter Science (CEMS)

## Biography

Hai Wang is now a Postdoctoral Researcher at Emergent Molecular Electronics Research Group (Watanabe Group), RIKEN Center for Emergent Matter Science. His research theme is efficient doping on conjugated polymers via chemical, electrochemical and photocatalytic techniques.

## Selected Publications

* H.Wang, K.Feng, T.Kaneta, et al. “Coherent Charge Transport Enhanced by Programmed Electrochemical Doping in Conjugated Polymers.” Small Methods (2026).
