<h2>Open Positions</h2>

### Postdoctoral Researchers

We are always seeking highly motivated postdoctoral researchers to join our team.

Candidates interested in organic electronics, molecular electronics, electrochemical doping, charge transport, advanced microscopy, soft materials, and related areas are encouraged to contact the PI directly by email to discuss potential opportunities.


---

### JSPS Postdoctoral Fellowship for Research in Japan (International Fellows)

We are happy to host applicants for the JSPS Postdoctoral Fellowship for Research in Japan.

Typical deadlines:

- Late April
- Late August

Please contact the PI several months before the application deadline.

Official website: [JSPS Postdoctoral Fellowship for Research in Japan](https://www.jsps.go.jp/english/e-fellow/e-ippan/index.html)

Application guidelines: [JSPS Application Guidelines and Schedule](https://www.jsps.go.jp/english/e-fellow/e-ippan/appliguidelines.html)

---

### JSPS Research Fellowship for Young Scientists (PD)

We welcome applicants who wish to apply for the JSPS PD Fellowship with Watanabe Laboratory as the host laboratory.

Typical application period:

- May–June

Applicants are strongly encouraged to contact the PI well before the application period.

Official website: [JSPS Research Fellowship for Young Scientists](https://www.jsps.go.jp/english/e-pd/)

---

### RIKEN Special Postdoctoral Researcher (SPDR)

Watanabe Laboratory welcomes prospective applicants to the RIKEN Special Postdoctoral Researcher Program.

The SPDR Program supports highly independent early-career researchers at RIKEN with competitive salary and research funding.

Typical application period:

- February–April
- FY2027 deadline: April 9, 2026

Prospective applicants are encouraged to contact the PI well before the application deadline.

Official website: [RIKEN Special Postdoctoral Researcher Program](https://www.riken.jp/en/careers/programs/spdr/)

FY2027 Call for Applications: [RIKEN SPDR FY2027 Call for Applications](https://www.riken.jp/en/careers/programs/spdr/career2027/)
