# Research

The Watanabe Laboratory studies emergent molecular electronics based on organic semiconductors, electrochemical doping, ion-electron coupling, and real-space measurements.

## Research Themes

### Multi-Carrier Charge Science

We explore the emergent duality between electronic and ionic charge carriers in conjugated molecular systems.

### Electrochemical Doping

We develop precise methods to control carrier density, counter-ion distribution, and transport properties in organic semiconductors.

### Quantum Organic Electronics

We investigate low-temperature transport, Hall effect, and quantum phenomena in soft molecular materials.

### Real-space Molecular Electronics

We combine advanced microscopy, nanofabrication, and electrical measurements to visualize structure-property relationships.
